Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is StreamLine?

Our project here at Group B, seeking to help you streamline the streaming services you're subscribed to.


Q: What should I watch next?

At this time, StreamLine does not recommend what you should binge next, but we're working on it!


Q: Where do you get your data from?

We use the Utelly API to find where the movies and shows on your watchlists are located. To get poster images and other information, we use TMDb along with information embedded in IMDb downloaded watchlists.

We also scrape websites such as the Google Play Store and iTunes to get pricing information
